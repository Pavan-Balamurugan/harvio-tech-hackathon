# Presentation Notes

## Slide 1: Problem

Educational institutions, hostels, and small businesses face growing cyber threats through links, QR codes, and document files.

## Slide 2: Gap

Existing tools are often expensive, complex, or designed for only one threat type.

## Slide 3: Solution

CyberShield is a low-cost web-based platform that scans multiple input formats, scores threats, and alerts administrators in real time.

## Slide 4: Demo

Show three quick flows:

- scan a URL
- scan a QR code
- upload a document

Then display the dashboard result.

## Slide 5: Impact

CyberShield improves cyber safety for institutions with limited resources and can be expanded into a larger security platform in the future.
